Title: Ordinary Things

----

Description: Embrace the ordinary, that’s where the beauty is

----

Date: 2023-08-23

----

Text:

Of 10 things that genuinely make me happy, 7 are ordinary day-to-day things.

- The first ray of sun hitting my face
- Taking a stroll in the neighborhood
- Grabbing coffee from the same local shop
- Humming to my favorite songs

Embrace the ordinary, that’s where the beauty is.
